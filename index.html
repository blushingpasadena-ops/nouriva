Before You Upload
What This Feature Does
Mivara's Lab Tracker lets you photograph or upload a PDF of your personal lab results. Our AI reads the document to extract biomarker names, values, and units — and nothing else.

What We Extract & Store
We extract ONLY: biomarker name (e.g., "LDL Cholesterol"), numeric value (e.g., "127"), and unit (e.g., "mg/dL"). We do NOT extract or store your name, date of birth, provider name, clinic name, address, account number, or any other identifying information.

Where Your Data Goes
Your uploaded image or PDF is sent to Google Gemini AI for text extraction only. It is not stored on Mivara's servers. The extracted biomarker values are saved locally on your device only — they never leave your device unless you explicitly export them using the Download My Data feature.

How We Use It
Your saved biomarker values are used solely to personalize the wellness suggestions shown in your Lab Tracker and daily habit reminders. They are never sold, shared, or used for advertising.

Your Rights
You can delete individual biomarker values or all your lab data at any time from the Lab Tracker tab or Settings > Privacy. Deletion is immediate and permanent.

Not Medical Advice
Mivara is a personal wellness self-tracker. It is not a medical provider, health plan, or healthcare clearinghouse. Nothing in this app constitutes medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always consult your healthcare provider to interpret your lab results.
